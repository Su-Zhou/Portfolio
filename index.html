About Me

Introduction

My name is Susane, but I go by the online alias Kusuri for my digital artworks. I am a student currently attending SAIT for the Interactive Design program with a specialization in UX/UI. I am mostly used to being an artist rather than a designer or problem solver so this education will be a unique experience throughouht my career journey. In my free time is when I like to make fanart of my favourite anime characters/video game characters or my favourite Virtual Youtubers. I also have begun to dive a bit into video editing using Davinci Resolve and now Adobe After Effects.

Programs I Use Most

  1. Clip Studio Paint
  2. Adobe Photoshop
  3. Adobe Illustrator

My Hobbies & Interest

Piano & Music
I have been playing piano for 10+ years. I also have dipped my toes into playing trumpet for band, and a bit of guitar during junior high. I'm pretty open to most music genres but I do have a preference to Jpop, J-Rock, Symphonic Rock, Musical-esque songs and especially Video Game soundtracks. Some of my favourite video game soundtracks include the NieR soundtrack, Tales of Xillia 1-2, the Pokemon Series, the Final Fantasy franchise, Chrono Trigger and Chrono Cross.
Video Games
I grew up playing and being surrouded by JRPGs rather than other video game genres. Some of my favourite franchises include: NieR:Automata, Nier:Replicant, Persona 3 & 4, the Tales of Series, the Trails series and Final Fantasy. I would talk about more, but the list could be endless at this rate!
Anime & Manga
I grew up watching anime and reading a bit of manga. Thanks to my older brother, this interest of mine has stuck with me since as long as I could remember things. I'm open to most genres and will give most genres a try but I do have a preference to the Shoujo manga and romances in general! Recently I've been getting into the Gundam series which has been a long, but fun ride. I even started making gunpla model kits recently too.
