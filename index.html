About Me

Introduction

My name is Susane Zhou, but I go by the online alias Kusuri for my digital artworks. I am a student currently attending SAIT for the Interactive Design program with a specialization in UX/UI. I am mostly used to being an artist rather than a designer or problem solver so this education will be a unique experience throughouht my career journey.
